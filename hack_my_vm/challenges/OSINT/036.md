https://hackmyvm.eu/challenges/challenge.php?c=036

# Concept
To Find BSSID of an open source public wifi network using wigle.net

# Method of Solve
- Go to the challenge website and download the image of the place
- search the place and look for pinpoint address of the place
- Then search the address on wigle.net to get the BSSID of the public wifi network the mall has
- Submit the BSSID as the flag
- The flag is **HMV{00:3A:9A:7B:5F:40}**
